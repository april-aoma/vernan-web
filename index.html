Leaderboard Crashes

Click the canvas for keyboard focus. Move with arrows/WASD, jump Z/Space, attack X, crouch Down, climb ladders with Up/Down. Stand in a door and press Up/W to change rooms. Pause with Enter/Esc, then Q to submit & quit (also available on the death screen). F3 toggles debug. Seed via ?seed=12345. On phones: Fullscreen, or Add to Home Screen for a standalone app.
